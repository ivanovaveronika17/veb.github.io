Kate Baldwin

Kate is the proud owner of The Holy Touch, a premier beauty salon located in the heart of Miami. With over a decade of experience in the beauty industry, Kate combines her passion for aesthetics with a keen business acumen to create a luxurious and welcoming environment for her clients. She is a certified cosmetologist and has trained with some of the top professionals in the field. Under her leadership, The Holy Touch specializes in nail care, makeup artistry, and waxing services, all delivered with the highest standards of quality and care.

Emily Davenport

With over five years of experience in the nail industry, Emily Davenport specializes in intricate nail art and gel manicures. She has a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating unique designs that cater to her clients' individual styles. Emily frequently attends workshops to stay updated with the latest trends and techniques.

Olivia Klein

Olivia is a certified nail technician with a focus on natural nail care and health. She excels in providing relaxing and rejuvenating manicure and pedicure treatments. Olivia's commitment to using high-quality, non-toxic products has made her a favorite among clients who prioritize both beauty and wellness.

Sarah Whitman

Sarah has been a professional makeup artist for eight years, working primarily in bridal and special event makeup. Her ability to enhance her clients' natural beauty while creating stunning, camera-ready looks has earned her a loyal following. Sarah is also skilled in makeup for photoshoots and editorial work, where her creativity shines.

Mia Hawthorne

Mia, a makeup artist with six years of experience, specializes in high-fashion and avant-garde makeup. She has collaborated with numerous designers and photographers, bringing bold and innovative looks to life. Mia's artistic approach and attention to detail have made her a standout in the competitive world of fashion makeup.

Isabella Sinclair

Isabella is a seasoned wax technician with a decade of experience in providing gentle and effective hair removal services. She is known for her precision and ability to make clients feel comfortable during their appointments. Isabella's expertise in various waxing techniques ensures smooth and long-lasting results for her clients.

Amelia Winslow

Amelia has been a dedicated wax technician for over seven years, specializing in Brazilian and full-body waxing. Her warm demeanor and professional skills help put clients at ease, making the waxing process as painless as possible. Amelia is committed to maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and customer satisfaction in her practice.
